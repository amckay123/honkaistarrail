Penacony


Penocony is the "land of dreams". The Astral Express get an invite from The Family- a super important organization that pretty much runs Penacony- and you go and see what the heck is going on. Penacony used to actually be a prison run by the Interastral Peace Corporation- the IPC-, but a stellaron magically appeared and the prisoners revolted against the IPC, gaining freedom.
