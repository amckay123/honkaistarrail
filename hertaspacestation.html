Story 1


Herta Space Station


This is the very first story you do in the game.
The Herta Space Station is a research ship founded by Madam Herta, member #83 of the Genius Society- an elusive club for really smart individuals. On the Herta Space Station, you get to meet lots of cool people- including Ruan Mei, another member of the Genius Society- and help save the space station from the bad guys, the Anti-Matter Legion.
