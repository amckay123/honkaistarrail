Story 2


Jarilo VI


Jarilo VI is the second story you complete. It is the first planet you land on as a member of the Astral Express.
In Jarilo VI, the two cities, the Overworld and Belobog-aka the Undercity. The elevators between the two cities are mysteriously shut down by the Overworld, and the people of Belobog are angry and confused. In this story arc, you unravel the secrets of Jarilo VI and battle your way through the corrupt government.
